What is it?

Not everything that Glitters is Gold, you may be wondering what it is, I’ll tell you.
This is an album, EP or whatever, by my friend Impul$e.
He released it in the month of February and since then, I’m yet to hear anything as beautiful.
This page is a tribute from me, not written by the artist, but rather an expression of what I feel about this guy’s music.

He’s got a great personality and that is reflected in his art.
My personal favourite song is the 3rd song – Audacious. This is not the first album he has delivered for us, there is a lot more on his soundcloud, and this is all shared in the discography.

Album description

My opinion on what this album means, is a summation of the youth in our times.
“Not everything that glitters is gold”, that’s just it, we see our lives as a joyful experience on a day to day basis while admiring what seems to be the greener grass.
The question really is, is it all gold?
Track number one, tell me what you what, outlines the fact that our generation is not straightforward more especially when it comes to love and hook-ups.
Track 2, self-explanatory we all have our pleasures and desires that keep us going, and for Impul$e, undeniably is mixing hot songs for us.
Track 3, my fav, extends track 1 and 2, Being straightforward in what we want with a mixture of how we all want to fulfil our inner pleasure, that of Love.
I’ll let you decide how the rest of the album makes you feel. Give it a try!

About March 11

One of the most beautiful nights….
We attended a listening party for the album. A few artists performed before he took centre stage.
The night was magnificent, friends and family got together to appreciate the good fruits of Impul$e’s labour.
I had never seen as many people celebrating local music in that manner.
The most beautiful thing is, we were not just vibing with him because he is our friend, but because the music was so good.
